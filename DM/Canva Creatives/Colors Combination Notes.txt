Color Combinatino Notes

Colors for Background, Primary Heading, Secondary Heading (Subheading), Paragraphy.


1. White and Blue
-> #F3F1FF (Background)
-> #352B5A (Primary Heading)

2. Blue, White & Gray
-> #0D0C18 (Background)
-> #FFFFFF (Heading)
-> #B0BAD6 (Heading with little contrast) mainly used for words in heading which are not that important as MAIN HEADING WORDS
-> #D2DDFE (Paragraphy)

3. Blue & Yellow (Own Palette)
-> #0D0C18 (Background)
-> #FEBB1E (Heading or Subheading or paragraphy)
